Passion Projects

Scholastic Learning

Combining Curriculum and Demonstration Learning for Rapid Convergence in Sparse Reward Environments

Taking some inspiration from human education in schools and universities, I explore the benefits and drawbacks that a similar method of education would have on RL students, rather than Human ones. By combining Curriculum Learning with regular demonstrations, I am able to acheive convergence to optimal policies orders of magnitude faster than traditional RL algorithms on sparse reward environments.

Deep Q Learning Doom -- Ongoing

Implementing a rational agent using Deep Q-Learning to play the original Doom video game developed by iD Software. Understanding the vast game state space of Doom, we decided to utilize a neural network that estimated Q-Values of these complex game states which inherently provided us with staggering results. Throughout the project, we utilized a variety of technologies such as Nvidia CUDA Parrellization, TensorFlow, and PyTorch.

Quantum Computing Tutorials -- Ongoing
