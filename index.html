Deep Q Learning Doom -- Ongoing

Implementing a rational agent using Deep Q-Learning to play the original Doom video game developed by iD Software. Understanding the vast game state space of Doom, we decided to utilize a neural network that estimated Q-Values of these complex game states which inherently provided us with staggering results. Throughout the project, we utilized a variety of technologies such as Nvidia CUDA Parrellization, TensorFlow, and PyTorch.

Quantum Computing Tutorials -- Ongoing

I am using Microsofts Q# library to explore and explain what it means to 'Code' on a quantum computer. Furthermore, I am using Q# with inline explanations in comments to recreate some of the major solutions that quantum computing provides, including Quantum Teleportation, Superdense Coding, and Shor's Algorithm.

2 Player Quantum Games: Tic Tac Toe

Wrote a paper and built a demo to play and solve 2 player quantum games, specifically Quantum-Tic-Tac-Toe. See the attached paper and video presentation in repo.
